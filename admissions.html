Admission Information

We welcome students who are eager to learn and grow. Our admission process is simple and designed to help parents and students easily enroll.

Admission Process

1. Submit Form

Fill out the admission form with accurate details.

2. Interview

Student and parents may be called for a short interview.

3. Confirmation

Selected students will receive admission confirmation.

Requirements

  • Birth Certificate
  • Previous School Record
  • Passport Size Photographs
  • Parent/Guardian CNIC Copy
